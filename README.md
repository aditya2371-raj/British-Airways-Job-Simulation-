# British-Airways-Job-Simulation-
This project simulates a real-world scenario for British Airways, focusing on analyzing customer booking behavior to understand and predict future purchasing patterns. The objective was to analyze customer booking data and build predictive models to identify factors that influence whether a customer will complete a booking.

Key Features

Data Exploration:
Performed in-depth exploratory data analysis on customer booking behavior data to identify patterns, trends, and key factors that influence booking decisions.

Customer Insights:
Analyzed customer interaction and booking-related features to understand behavioral patterns and determine the factors that impact the likelihood of a customer making a purchase.

Predictive Modeling:
Developed two machine learning models — Logistic Regression and Random Forest — to predict whether a customer will complete a booking in the future.

Model Evaluation:
Compared the performance of Logistic Regression and Random Forest models to determine the most effective approach for predicting customer buying behavior and supporting data-driven decision making.
